Favorite Food Recipe Collection

Recipes, notes, and experiments in cooking

Site Name

Favorite Food Recipe Collection — A personal collection of my favorite recipes, notes, and experiments in cooking. The name communicates the site's focus on specific recipes rather than a blog or ad-driven site.

Site Purpose

The site provides a searchable collection of recipes with ingredients, step-by-step instructions, personal notes (tips/tricks), and a gallery of completed dishes. It also includes a simple form to add new recipes and a highlight area for recent favorites.

Scenarios

  • As a weekday cook, I need a quick recipe with affordable ingredients I can prepare in under 45 minutes.
  • As an experimenter, I want clear notes and tips on techniques (stuff like pastrami brine, sous-vide times) so I can reproduce the result.

Color Schema

Selected colors and usage:

  • Primary: used for headings, primary buttons, and accents. Color: #264653
  • Accent: used for highlights, logo, and call-to-action elements. Color: #E76F51
  • Neutral: background and body text contrast. Color: #F7F7F7

Typography

Fonts and usage:

  • Playfair Display — Headings and display text
  • Source Sans Pro — Body text, captions, navigation, forms, etc.
